Salary Transparency

This page will list every salary for every single job I've had in tech. I've never done this before but after seeing all of the talks around salary and transparency, I'm laying it all out there on the table for everyone to see. My hope is that others will do the same and, at the very least, feel empowered to negotiate their salaries.
