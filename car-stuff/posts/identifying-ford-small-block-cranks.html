Identifying Ford Small Block Cranks

Crankshaft in hand you're wondering to yourself, what flippin rotating assembly is this part of? 289? 302? Sure you could measure the stroke with a caliper, but if you've got the engine apart right now, odds are that you're not wanting to put it together to measure the stroke.So just check the casting numbers! Also, to give credit where it's due this is where I got the crankshaft casting number data.
