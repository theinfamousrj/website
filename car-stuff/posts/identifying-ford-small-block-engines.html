Identifying Ford Small Block Engines

So here you are with your engine and you have no idea what you've got outside of what you were told by whoever you purchased this from and what you can see for yourself: straight-six, v8, etc. So I spent a lot of time on the internet digging for information and compiling it in a quick-access format. If you just search this page for the first 3 characters of your casting number, you should be able to find your engine pretty easily. You can also use the handy little test Summit Racing built. Also, be aware that this only covers engines from 1952-1996 and this is where I got the engine block code data.
